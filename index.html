Current and recent projects.

Cards with a Cause

'Northern Cats', is a greetings card range featuring North East legends in cat form. A fun and educational tribute to Northern heritage.

The project also aims to raise awareness of rescue cats in need of homes and encourage responsible pet ownership. Every year the RSPC rescues over 20,000 cats in the UK.

The Village Cat

'The Village Cat', was a greetings card range inspired by the cats who live in the villages of Durham.

Owners were invited to submit a photograph of their pets to be featured on the cards. I transformed each photograph into a life-like illustration, and matched this with a memorable quote.
